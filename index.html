A PhD student. Apolitical. A virgin. Forced to go all the way in the opposite directions. Will his women friends help him with Magic and Kabbalah?

His signature will change the world. He has the right face to do it. He has every reason to sign. And every reason not to.

He has just 11 days. On his dangerous and steamy ride: an ancient battle between Black and White Witches with erotic weapons. The carnal secrets of The Book of Exodus that Moses himself did not anticipate. Mathematical and social science riddles with real consequences. And desire — specific, burning, passionate, and physical.

Sex Tax is a dark romantic thriller with even more heat and more intelligence than the title suggests.

About the Author

Zak Maymin is an award-winning and best-selling author with a Ph.D. in mathematics from MIT. He has published fiction and non-fiction for children and adults on ethical and moral issues, using mathematical training and first-principles thinking to analyze social problems through the Socratic method.

Before he started writing books, he was a professor, portfolio manager, and chess player.

As an immigrant from Russia, Zak recognizes the warning signs of a country turning itself into a totalitarian state — and writes about what that looks like from the inside.

He has many children and grandchildren and lives in Connecticut.
